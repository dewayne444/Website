About the Project

At the heart of many of the world's most pressing issues—climate change, poverty, and discrimination—is a fundamental problem: the human mindset.

People often behave destructively not due to a lack of resources or opportunity, but because of how they think. For example, trees are cut down and plastics are dumped not always out of necessity, but out of carelessness and short-term thinking. Even when solutions are available, if people don't see how those solutions benefit them personally, they rarely act.

Much like someone addicted to drugs resists help because they believe it's working for them, many resist change unless shown how it benefits them. Real change begins with mindset transformation, and that starts by showing people clear, direct benefits from acting differently.

What We Offer

Plastic-to-Crypto

To change behavior around plastic waste, we need to treat it like a valuable commodity—just as iron is hardly seen littered in Nigeria because people know it can be sold.

That's the foundation for VBRM, a revolutionary solution that incentivizes plastic recycling using digital currency. Here's how it works:

  • People bring plastic waste to authorized VBRM centers
  • Machines weigh plastics and display real-time value
  • Users receive blockchain-enabled cards to enable them get their reward and to also be able to use it to buy or order online, with the cryptocurrency they get from the system
  • All transactions are securely recorded on the blockchain
Mindset Change

Our program doesn't just clean up waste—it transforms how people think about resources and their environment.

By linking behavior directly to income, we demonstrate that motivation and reward go hand-in-hand, creating lasting change in community attitudes toward sustainability.

Educational programs reinforce the connection between environmental responsibility and personal benefit, ensuring long-term adoption of sustainable practices.

Jobs & Skills

VBRM creates multiple income streams throughout the recycling ecosystem:

  • Collection agents who gather plastics from communities
  • Processing staff who prepare materials for recycling
  • Technicians who maintain our blockchain-enabled systems
  • Community educators who spread awareness

We provide training at every level, creating not just jobs but career paths in the green economy.

Pollution in Focus

The harsh reality of plastic pollution and its impact on our planet

Urban Plastic Refuse

Mountains of plastic waste accumulating in urban areas, overwhelming local waste management systems.

Ocean Plastic Soup

Our oceans have become dumping grounds, with plastic outnumbering marine life in some areas.

River Plastic Highways

Waterways transport plastic waste from inland areas to the oceans, creating floating garbage patches.

Landfill Overflow

Traditional waste management systems are overwhelmed by the volume of non-biodegradable plastic waste.

Marine Life Impact

Over 1 million marine animals die each year from plastic pollution through ingestion and entanglement.

Community Dumping

Improper disposal practices turn neighborhoods into plastic wastelands, creating health hazards.

Did you know? Every minute, the equivalent of one garbage truck of plastic is dumped into our oceans.

Democratizing Finance with Circle Wallet

VBRM introduces millions to blockchain technology through the most accessible gateway - Circle Wallet and USDC stablecoin. Just as people trust traditional banking, we're building that same familiarity with digital finance.

Accessible to All

Whether young or old, educated or not - the VBRM system automatically creates Circle Wallets for users. The simplicity of plastic-to-crypto conversion makes blockchain tangible for everyone.

Stable & Trustworthy

Using USDC ensures users aren't exposed to crypto volatility. Like knowing your bank balance won't fluctuate overnight, people gain confidence in this stable digital currency.

Gateway to Web3

This creates the first touchpoint for millions to enter the digital economy. After experiencing USDC through plastic recycling, users naturally explore other blockchain applications.
